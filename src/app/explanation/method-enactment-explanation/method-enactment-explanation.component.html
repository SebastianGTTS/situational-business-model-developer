Method Enactment

The third stage of Method Enactment is used by the Business Developer to enact a development method. This can be done based on a Composed Development Method or an Ad-hoc Created Development Method.

The Composed Development Method enacts a predefined development method and executes the predefined developments steps. Here, a already Composed Development Method is selected and configured as the current one. Each of the predefined Method Building Blocks is executed as development step to develop the business model. During the enactment of the development method, a change of the composed development method might be requested.

The Ad-hoc Created Development Method creates a new development method and executes the flexible development steps. Here, a New Development Method is created by choosing the situational factors and the application domains. Based on that, new Method Building Blocks are flexible selected in an ad-hoc manner from the repository. The blocks are executed as development steps to develop the business model.
